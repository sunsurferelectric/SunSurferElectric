Our Story

Sun Surfer Electric

"Going solar isn't just about pulling dollars from the sky. It's about being part of an energy shift that's already underway — and we're building it one panel, one battery, one household at a time."

I'm Mack Mical, and I've spent over 20 years in electrical construction — residential, commercial, industrial, controls, lighting, and for the last decade, solar. I got my start in the trade in 2005, the same year my son Zonny was born. Funny how that worked out.

In 2023, Zonny joined me as my apprentice at a local solar company. For two years we installed and serviced residential and commercial systems side by side, and I taught him everything I knew — not just the code and the wiring, but the why behind it.

By 2025, we knew it was time to build something of our own. That's how Sun Surfer Electric was born.

And solar didn't stay confined to the job site. We wired our garden for solar-powered water filtration and automatic irrigation. We built a solar generator for camping trips. We even built a solar-powered stage for our band — because once you start seeing the sun as a power source, you start finding reasons to use it everywhere. When you hire us, you're not getting a crew that shows up, installs a system, and disappears. You're getting two guys who actually believe in what they're building, because we've already built it for ourselves.

That's the passion we bring to every job — and we'd love to bring it to your home next.

Mack & Zonny Mical Founders, Sun Surfer Electric
